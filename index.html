About

Saundra Jones
Principal Designer

Circle/Square Design is a graphic design studio founded by Saundra Jones. The studio delivers strategic, visually compelling design solutions across a broad range of disciplines — brand identity, print collateral, editorial design, environmental graphics, event design, and digital media.

With clients spanning healthcare, real estate, nonprofit, government, and the arts, Circle/Square brings the same thoughtful, detail-oriented approach to every project, from a logo to a full environmental installation.

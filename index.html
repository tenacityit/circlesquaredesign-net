About

CircleSquare llc is a creative collaboration of Saundra Jones and a team of talented independent designers, photographers, web designers and production experts assembled to support your marketing needs with award-winning design. We offer design and creative for a variety of sectors:

commercial real estate  •  start-up companies  •  economic development  •  health care  •  education  •  non-profits  •  tourism  •  private industry  •  state & local government

Successfully making the connection between good design and an effective message, Saundra is an award-winning graphic designer with 25+ years experience. Saundra is a MICA (Maryland Institute College of Arts) Magna Cum Laude graduate and lover of all things creative from fine arts, design, performing arts, architecture and everything in between.
